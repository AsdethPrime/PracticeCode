DeviceAdminInfo class is used to specify metadata information of a device admin component.

It contacts the following policies

2. DeviceAdminInfo.USES_POLICY_DISABLE_CAMERA
3. DeviceAdminInfo.USES_POLICY_DISABLE_KEYGUARD_FEATURES
4. DeviceAdminInfo.USES_POLICY_EXPIRE_PASSWORD
5. DeviceAdminInfo.USES_POLICY_FORCE_LOCK
6. DeviceAdminInfo.USES_POLICY_LIMIT_PASSWORD
7. DeviceAdminInfo.USES_POLICY_RESET_PASSWORD
8. DeviceAdminInfo.USES_POLICY_WATCH_LOGIN
