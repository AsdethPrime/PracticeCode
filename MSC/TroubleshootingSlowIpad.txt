Some general tips for troubleshooting slow ipad

1. Check Memory usages:
Settings > General > Storage and iCloud > Manage Storage

2. Reset all your settings:
Settings> General > Reset > Reset all settings

3. Wipe cache:
Settings> Safari
> clear history, cookies and data

4. Turn off background app refresh
Settings > General > Background App Refresh > Toggle Switch Off

5. Reducred graphics and animation
Settings > General > Accessibility > Reduced Motion > Toggle Switch Off
